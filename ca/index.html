About
Campus Ambassador


Aaruush is SRMIST's National Level Techno Management Fest. It has a footfall over more than 60000 people and conducts 60+ events along with workshops, technical exhibitions, highlights, PRO shows and much more.

The campus ambassor of Aaruush holds a plethora of opportunities for the individual. The idea behind this is to connect the brilliant minds of the nation together from different parts of the country. Along with this the campus ambassador gets goodies and an experience of a lifetime.

Prerequisites


1. Good communication skills.
2. Good knowledge about their college.
3. Good contact with teachers and people of their college.
4. Should work hard and efficiently during their hour.
5. Capable of representing the views and notices of CA.



Terms
&
Conditions






1. The Certification of Organization as College Representative will be given only if he/she fulfills the necessary conditions and is deemed fit by the organisers.

2. Any change in the reward scheme before or after the selection of
ambassador is under the sole discretion of Aaruush. However the changes would be notified to the campus abassador at the earliest for their convenience.

3. Only share from CA portal will be counted as points. So it is recommended to share from CA portal rather than directly from facebook.

4. Shares from CA portal should be public to make it count as points.
